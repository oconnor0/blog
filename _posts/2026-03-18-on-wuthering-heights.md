title: "on wuthering heights"
categories: fiction poetry

```
heathcliff stole
ole earnshaw's love
above this slight
might hindley rise
wise and embrace heathcliff
miff was hindley now
plow to fill his days
ways to be flogged anon
on and on he ran
can he return a man
plan within his mind
kind never again
```
